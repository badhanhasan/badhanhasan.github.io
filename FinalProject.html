Final Project

Connect4


Hello! Welcome to my final project code. My partner Colin O'Connor and I coded a full working connect 4 game for CSC 106 at URI. The game has a splash screen, a game screen, and a win screen. After clicking the start button on the splash screen you can start to play the game. The way you play it is by connecting 4 discs of the same color either vertically, horizontally, or diagonally. Once you have 4 discs connected, you will be brought to the win screen where you will see what color won. You can restart the game and go back to the splash screen by clicking the restart button. Hope you enjoy it!
